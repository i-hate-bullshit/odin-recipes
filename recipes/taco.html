Taco

Description

This is my go-to fish taco sauce. You can make it ahead and keep it in the fridge. You can substitute any hot sauce for the Sriracha.

Ingredients

  • 3 tablespoons sour cream
  • 2 tablespoons mayonnaise
  • 1 tablespoon lime juice
  • 1/2 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon Sriracha sauce

Steps

  1. Fry meat
  2. Mix sour cream, mayonnaise, lime juice, garlic powder, and Sriracha together in a bowl until smooth.
  3. Put cheese on tortilla
  4. Put mix on tortilla
  5. Put meat on tortilla
